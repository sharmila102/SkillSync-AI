About SkillSync AI

Empowering Tech Talents to Align with Industry Standards

SkillSync AI was engineered to solve a common problem: candidates don't know exactly why they fail candidate filters or what skills they need to acquire to land specific technical roles.

By integrating document parsing with smart gap calculations, SkillSync AI helps developers plan their upskilling paths and prove their progress with charts, roadmaps, and portfolio projects.
